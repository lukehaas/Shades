# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2025**

## Overview

Shades is a browser extension that applies color filter overlays to websites. Your privacy is important to us, and Shades is designed with privacy in mind.

## Data Collection

Shades does **not** collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Website content
- Usage analytics
- Cookies or tracking data

## Local Storage

Shades uses Chrome's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`) to save your preferences directly on your device. This data includes:

- Your filter settings for individual websites
- Your default filter preferences
- Extension enabled/disabled state

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Permissions

Shades requires the following permissions to function:

- **Storage**: To save your filter preferences locally on your device
- **ActiveTab**: To apply filters to the current webpage
- **Tabs**: To detect the current website domain for applying saved filters

These permissions are used solely to provide the core functionality of the extension.

## Third-Party Services

Shades does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external servers.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
